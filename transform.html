My Process for this image

For this image, I mainly used the "colors" tab in GIMP. I changed things around like the brightness, contrast, exposure, and color temperature. I just mostly experimented to see what looked right, and undid any changes I made that I wasn't satisfied with. The result gives this beautiful cat smoother lighting and more contrast, so you can see his cute face better.
