Sleepy Kitty Drawing

My Process for this image

First, I found an image that I wanted to draw. I then traced over the kitty in the image with my Surface Pen, and traced the outline of the ledge he is sleeping on in a different layer. I added my own details like the bushes, the bricks on the house, the cobblestone foundation and the windowsill. Lastly, I added color layers- one for the cat, and one for the surroundings. I used the freehand select tool to fill in areas with different colors, and then used the pen and blur tools to smooth out edges and create a decent drawing. The window and the gray ledge are also gradients. I'm pretty proud of the final result, although if I had more time, I'd add more details.
